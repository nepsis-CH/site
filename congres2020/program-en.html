Workshops
  • Groups of 10 -15 participants each
  • There are 5 workshops running in parallel, each workshop runs twice (one after the other), each participant can register for 2 workshops
  • Workshop duration: 1h30 or 2h (this time includes the presentation of those who participate and the final break / switching to the next workshop)
  • Workshop schedule: Saturday after 3 p.m.

The themes of the 5 workshops are:

  • The Apostle Paul on the Christian in the City (the only one to be held in English)
  • Pateric / Philocal Circle about Life in the Citadel
  • Christian film club
  • Fake news
  • Pro-life workshop

Details of each workshop in the workshop registration form.
